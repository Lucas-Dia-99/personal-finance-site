Rent vs Buy Calculator

Compare renting and buying over 1–40 years. The model runs a monthly amortization, tracks equity, and includes property tax, homeowners insurance, maintenance, HOA, rent inflation, home appreciation, and selling costs.

Open the calculator

How we calculate

  • Owning net cost: cumulative outflows − equity (minus selling fees at exit).
  • Renting net cost: rent + renter’s insurance, inflated annually.
  • Principal reduces balance (not a cost); interest, taxes, insurance, maintenance, HOA are costs.

When to use this

Use for first-home decisions, move vs. stay comparisons, or to gauge how rate and price shifts affect the breakeven year.
